故事：鹿和葡萄 很久以前，有一只鹿在山林中漫游。一天，它发现了一片美味的葡萄园，葡萄悬挂在藤蔓上，看起来异常诱人。鹿闻到了葡萄的香气，立刻对它们产生了浓厚的兴趣。 然而，葡萄却挂得很高，鹿无法轻易够到。它试了很多次，跳跃、伸长脖子，但都无济于事。鹿感到非常沮丧，它开始嘲笑那些葡萄，说它们肯定是酸的，不值得自己费力去尝试。 就在鹿打算离开的时候，一个智慧的老狐狸出现了。老狐狸看到了鹿的尴尬和失落，于是走了过来。 “朋友，”老狐狸说道，“你为什么要嘲笑那些葡萄呢？它们或许真的很美味，只是你没有用心去尝试。” 鹿有些疑惑，但还是听从了老狐狸的建议。它闭上了眼睛，集中精神，然后跳起来，一次又一次地尝试够到葡萄。虽然仍然很难，但鹿却不再嘲笑葡萄，而是专心致志地努力。 终于，在几次尝试后，鹿成功地够到了一串葡萄。当它品尝到那甜美的味道时，欣喜若狂。它明白了一个道理，那就是只有付出努力和耐心，才能获得真正的回报。

Story: The Deer and the Grapes Long ago, in a distant forest, there lived a deer that roamed the woods. One day, it stumbled upon a vineyard filled with delicious grapes hanging from the vines, looking incredibly tempting. The aroma of the grapes reached the deer's nostrils, sparking its curiosity. However, the grapes were hanging high up, out of the deer's reach. It tried jumping and stretching its neck, but all its efforts were in vain. The deer felt frustrated and began to mock the grapes, claiming they must be sour and not worth the trouble. Just as the deer was about to leave, a wise old fox appeared. The old fox noticed the deer's embarrassment and disappointment, so it approached the deer. "Friend," the old fox said, "why do you mock those grapes? They might truly be delicious, only you haven't tried hard enough." The deer was puzzled, but it decided to heed the old fox's advice. Closing its eyes, the deer focused its energy and began to jump repeatedly, attempting to reach the grapes. Though still challenging, the deer stopped mocking the grapes and concentrated diligently. Finally, after several attempts, the deer managed to grasp a bunch of grapes. As it savored the sweet taste, it felt elated. It had learned a valuable lesson – that true rewards come from putting in effort and exercising patience.
