The deer felt frustrated and began to mock the grapes, claiming they must be sour and not worth the trouble. Just as the deer was about to leave, a wise old fox appeared. They might truly be delicious, only you haven't tried hard enough." The deer was puzzled, but it decided to heed the old fox's advice. Closing its eyes, the deer focused its energy and began to jump repeatedly, attempting to reach the grapes. Though still challenging, the deer stopped mocking the grapes and concentrated diligently. Finally, after several attempts, the deer managed to grasp a bunch of grapes. As it savored the sweet taste, it felt elated.
